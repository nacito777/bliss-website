About Bliss Catering

Discover our story, passion, and commitment to exceptional culinary experiences

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Bliss Catering began as a small family business with a big dream: to bring exceptional culinary experiences to every celebration. What started in a humble kitchen has grown into one of Ghana's most trusted catering services.

Our passion for food and dedication to excellence has driven us to serve thousands of satisfied customers across Accra and beyond. From intimate dinner parties to grand wedding celebrations, we've been privileged to be part of life's most precious moments.

Our Mission

To create unforgettable culinary experiences that bring people together, using the finest ingredients and exceptional service to make every event special.

Our Values
